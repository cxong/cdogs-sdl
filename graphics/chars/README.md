- Rows are:
  - Groups of 5 rows: unarmed/armed
  - First row of group: idle
  - Next 4 rows of group: walking
- Columns are directions
- Colors are:
  - Red (0 hue): skin
  - Green (120 hue): hair
  - Blue (240 hue): arms
  - Yellow (60 hue): body
  - Cyan (180 hue): legs
  - Any other shade: as is (recommend R, G, and B between 5 and 250)
